Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Negi's

What is Negi's ?

▼

Negi's is an online learning platform offering comprehensive study materials, video lessons, practice tests, and a community for students in classes 6-10.

How do I sign up?

▼

Click on the Login button in the top right corner. You can create a new account by entering your email and setting a password.

Are the courses free?

▼

Many courses and study materials are available for free. We also offer premium courses and resources available for purchase in our shop.

Can I download the study materials?

▼

Yes, you can download PDFs of study notes directly from our Notes section. Videos can be streamed online.

What payment methods do you accept?

▼

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, and digital wallet payments. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

How can I track my progress?

▼

Visit the Achievements section to view your completed tests, earned badges, and certificates. Your profile page shows all your learning activity.

Is there a community forum?

▼

Yes! Join our Community section to connect with other students, ask questions, and get peer support on various topics.

How do I get support?

▼

You can reach us through our Contact page, email support@Zentegix.com, or use the chat support available on the platform.
